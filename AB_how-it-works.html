How AntBarter Works

  1. Sign Up: Sign Up and complete your profile creation.
  2. Create a Barter Posting: Showcase your goods and services for other professionals near you to discover.
  3. Find a Barter Posting: Found a good or service that you are looking for? Connect with the professional.
  4. Barter Agreement & Exchange: Complete a secure barter agreement and carry out the exchange.
  5. Provide Feedback: Leave a review to share your experience.
